The Official Liverpool Fc Store

Being a Liverpool fan I was so happy to get the opportunity to do the pitch designs for the official online store of Liverpool FC. The catch was I had 2 days to turn around a homepage for desktop, tablet and mobile along with a product page for the kit.

After winning the pitch, Liverpool liked the designs so much that what we pitched essentially became the design for the actual site. the only change being in the header where their club partner needed to be included.

This project gave me huge confidence in my own ability. The thought that designs I did in 2 days can grace one of the biggest football clubs stores going makes me very proud!

Everton FC Blue Crimbo Microsite

Below are the designs and illustration I did for the Everton FC christmas campaign. The idea behind the site was the more users engaged by uploading a picture, posting a tweet or creating a stocking, the more the illustrated map would gradually turn blue.

The idea behind the stocking game was that users could select from a number of products in the shop to add to their stocking. The only criteria was that it couldn't be more than £200. The user could then share their stocking with their friends and get entered into a prize draw to win the entire contents of their created stocking
